About EcoFinds

EcoFinds is a second-hand marketplace built during a hackathon. Our mission is simple: extend product lifecycles and make sustainable consumption easier for everyone.

How it works

  • ✨ Sign up & log in
  • 📷 Create product listings with images and specs
  • 🛒 Browse items, add to cart, and checkout (mock)

Why EcoFinds?

♻️ Sustainable: Reduce waste by giving items a second life.

💰 Affordable: Buy and sell at fair prices.

🤝 Community-driven: Connect with local buyers and sellers.
